## What is Smart Pixels?

Smart Pixels is an online AI portrait generator and editor that transforms everyday photos into professional, studio-grade portraits. It combines powerful portrait AI, intuitive settings, and ready‑to‑use templates so anyone can get natural, flattering results—without a studio session.

## What You Can Do

- Generate complete portrait sets for weddings, travel, street, ID photos, maternity, pets, and themed shoots
- Apply natural retouching: skin smoothing, lighting adjustment, background blur, face slimming, and more
- Switch between top‑tier image models to find the look you love
- Enhance prompts and receive editing suggestions for consistent, high‑quality output
- Download and share studio‑quality results in seconds

## Portrait Retouch Editor

Upload your photos, choose enhancements, and generate AI‑retouched portraits.

- Upload: up to 15MB per image; select multiple images
- Beauty presets: universal tweaks (blurred background, lighting supplement), face shape edits (face slimming, double chin), skin care (soften, brighten, wrinkle and blemish removal)
- Focus features: eyes (open eyes, dark circle removal, red‑eye fix), nose and mouth/teeth refinements, hair shine and softness
- Artifacts removal: glasses glare, tattoo remover
- Other edits: free‑form prompt for attire, posture, accessories, and more
- Models: Nano Banana AI and Seedream 4.0
- Status feedback: pending, processing, success, failed
