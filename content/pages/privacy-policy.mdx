## Introduction

Welcome to Smart Pixels, the **AI Portrait Generator & Editor** designed to help you transform everyday photos into professional, studio-quality portraits using advanced AI technology. At Smart Pixels, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI portrait generation and editing services on smart-pixels.com.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using smart-pixels.com:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Image Data**
   - **What We Collect**: Images you upload for transformation, processing parameters, and generated results.
   - **Purpose**: To perform AI portrait generation and editing, including portrait retouching, professional portrait photography sets, background adjustment, skin smoothing, face slimming, lighting enhancement, body editing, and style transformations.
   - **Storage**: Uploaded images are temporarily stored during processing and may be retained for service improvement purposes.

3. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use smart-pixels.com, including portrait generation types, processing settings, features accessed, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our AI models, and enhance our portrait generation and editing services.

4. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access smart-pixels.com, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

5. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our AI portrait generation and editing services.

## AI Processing and Image Data

Our AI portrait generation and editing services require processing of your uploaded images. Please note:

- **Processing Purpose**: Images are processed solely to provide the requested AI portrait generation and editing services.
- **Data Retention**: Processed images may be temporarily stored for service delivery and may be used to improve our AI models and service quality.
- **Quality Assurance**: We may analyze processing results to ensure service quality and accuracy.
- **No Unauthorized Use**: We do not use your images for any purpose other than providing the requested portrait services and improving our AI technology.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information and image data seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and uploaded images. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or image data to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
- To process images using third-party AI services that are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request access to your personal information and image data we hold.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your uploaded images and associated data.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Data Portability**: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

## Changes to This Policy

You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
